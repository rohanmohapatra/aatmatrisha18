ABOUT PES

PES University (formerly known as P.E.S. Institute of Technology) was established as a State Private University under Karnataka Act No. 16 of 2013 on November 19, 2013. The University offers Bachelors and Masters Programs in engineering, business management and computer applications. The vision of the University is to create professionally superior and ethically strong global work force. PES University is placed 7th on the list of INDIA'S MOST TRUSTED Educational Institutes (Source: TRA) and is in the list of Top 25 M.Tech colleges in India.



ABOUT US

AATMATRISHA,the annual cultural festival of PES University, has grown into being Bengaluru's hottest festival. What sets AATMATRISHA apart from the other festivals is the devotion of the students who work towards it to make it a memorable experience, and their urge to deliver the maximum. AATMATRISHA caters to a huge range of interests, all the way from shows by legends such as Raghu Dixit (2012), Sunidhi Chauhan (2015), Salim-Sulaiman (2016) and Vishal Shekhar (2017)during the "Pro-Nite" down to contests and competitions for students in the technical and cultural field.
